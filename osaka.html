Welcome to Osaka, the culinary capital of Japan!
Dive into a city alive with neon lights, bustling markets, and mouthwatering street food. Experience the warmth of its people, explore historic castles, and enjoy its vibrant nightlife.

Discover Osaka!

Discover Osaka

Osaka is known for its modern architecture, nightlife, and hearty street food. Here are some highlights you shouldn't miss:

Dotonbori
The heart of Osaka's nightlife.

Osaka Castle
A historic landmark with beautiful gardens.

Osaka Aquarium
One of the largest public aquariums in the world.

Namba Walk
An underground shopping street.

Sumiyoshi Taisha
One of Japan's oldest Shinto shrines.

Osaka Train
Efficient and convenient transportation.

Street Food
A variety of delicious options.

Matcha Tea
A traditional Japanese beverage.

Shitennoji Temple
One of Japan's oldest temples.

Kuromon Ichiba Market
A lively market known for fresh seafood.

Umeda Sky Building
A striking skyscraper with a rooftop observatory.

Takoyaki
A popular street food in Osaka.
