Welcome to Kyoto, the cultural heart of Japan!
Immerse yourself in a city rich with history, stunning temples, and beautiful gardens. Experience the tranquility of its tea houses, explore ancient shrines, and enjoy its traditional cuisine.

Discover Kyoto!

Discover Kyoto

Kyoto is known for its classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses.
Here are some highlights you shouldn't miss:

Kinkaku-ji
The Golden Pavilion, a Zen Buddhist temple.

Fushimi Inari Shrine
Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
A serene bamboo forest.

Gion District
Famous for its geisha and traditional tea houses.

Philosopher's Path
A scenic walking path along a canal.

Kaiseki Cuisine
Traditional multi-course Japanese dinner.

Tea Ceremony
Experience the traditional Japanese tea ceremony.

Nijo Castle
A flatland castle and UNESCO World Heritage site.

Kamo River
A beautiful river running through Kyoto.

Ryoan-ji
A Zen temple famous for its rock garden.

Yudofu
A traditional tofu hot pot dish.

Kyo-Yasai
Kyoto's traditional vegetables.
