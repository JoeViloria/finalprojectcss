Discover Tokyo

Tokyo is known for its modern architecture, shopping districts, and cultural landmarks. Here are some highlights you shouldn't miss:

Shibuya Crossing
The world's busiest pedestrian crossing.

Senso-ji Temple
Tokyo's oldest temple.

Tokyo Tower
A communications and observation tower.

Akihabara
Famous for its electronics and otaku culture.

Meiji Shrine
A Shinto shrine dedicated to Emperor Meiji.

Tsukiji Fish Market
The largest wholesale fish market in the world.

Odaiba
A high-tech entertainment hub on an artificial island.

Ueno Park
A spacious public park with museums and a zoo.

Roppongi Hills
A modern complex with shops, restaurants, and an observation deck.

Asakusa
A district known for its historic sites and traditional atmosphere.

Shinjuku Gyoen
A large park with beautiful gardens.

Ramen
A popular Japanese noodle soup dish.
